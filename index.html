About Us

Welcome to ,a family run business, located in Co.Galway. We pride ourselves on serving locally-sourced, organic food that is healthy AND tasty!
We offer over fifty different types of salads, pasta and rice dishes, so there is something for every taste bud.
Not only serving delicious(and nutritious) meals, we also offer the best coffee and tea selection, so you can enjoy everything for lunch or dinner in one convenient location.
Our super happy and friendly staff are more than willing to help you with your selection or if you have any dietary needs or requirements.

As an added benefit, we also offer outside catering for family gatherings or corporate events. Just fill in this form to let us know your details and we'll get back to you shortly.
